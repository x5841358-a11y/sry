Timeline

Tujhe toh pata hi hai hum first time kaise mile the. Dheere dheere hum friends bane, aur sach bolu toh mujhe pata hi nahi chala ki mujhe tujhse kab pyaar ho gaya. Main bas itna jaanta hoon ki main tujhse bohot zyada attach ho gaya.

Hum daily baat karte the, chhoti chhoti cheezein share karte the. Aaj tak main kisi se itna attach nahi hua, wo bhi bina physically mile. Haan, long distance thoda tough tha, par sach mein mujhe hamesha feel hua ki tu mere liye bohot important hai.

Shayad main tujhe utna samajh nahi paaya. Shayad maine tujhe frustrate kiya. Par sach mein yaar, maine apni taraf se poori koshish ki. Aur aaj bhi, main tere liye apna best dene ke liye ready hoon.

Mujhe yaad hai jab mere exams ke baad tune bola tha ki “mujhe NEET ke liye padhna hai”. Wo time mere liye bohot mushkil tha, par jab tu wapas aayi, tujhe idea bhi nahi hai main kitna khush tha.

Is baar mujhe lagta hai galti meri thi. Mujhe aisa nahi karna chahiye tha. Wo jaan-bujhkar nahi tha — maine kabhi kisi galat intention se login nahi kiya. Main maanta hoon, is baar fault mera tha.

Aur jo tune us moment par kiya, shayad wo sahi tha. Par sach ye hai — main aaj bhi tujhe utna hi pyaar karta hoon.

Apology

Dear Samruddhi,

I reacted out of fear, not facts. Accusing you was wrong, and I take full responsibility for that.

You were already dealing with so much, and instead of being calm and understanding, I let my emotions take over. That was my mistake.

This is not written to explain myself, justify anything, or reopen the past. It is simply to acknowledge where I went wrong.

You have always mattered to me, baby. And the last thing I ever wanted was to hurt you or make things heavier for you.

You don’t need to reply to this or do anything about it. I just wanted to say this honestly and respectfully.

Take care, my love.
